# Revenue Playbook v2.9

## First 10 Users Plan
1. Identify 30 relevant bot/agent builders on Base.
2. Send high-signal intros with API + proof links.
3. Target 10 pilot starts through low-friction onboarding.
4. Provide integration support and gather concrete outcome metrics.

## 100 Free Scan Pilot
- Offer up to 100 free scans per qualified team.
- Goal: prove signal usefulness and integration fit.
- Require one short feedback loop at pilot end.

## $50 Founder Pilot Option
- Offer a paid founder pilot at $50 for teams wanting direct setup support.
- Include onboarding call, initial policy mapping, and usage review.

## Converting to Paid Usage
1. Map pilot outcomes to lane economics.
2. Recommend a default paid lane based on call volume and urgency.
3. Set a clear upgrade point from pilot to recurring x402 usage.
4. Track retention by recurring paid calls and policy adoption depth.

## Manual Lead Tracking
- Keep a lightweight tracker with:
  - builder/team name
  - source channel
  - first contact date
  - pilot status
  - conversion status
  - next follow-up date

## Focus Rule
Do not launch any token/coin recommendation effort before recurring revenue and validated user retention are established.
