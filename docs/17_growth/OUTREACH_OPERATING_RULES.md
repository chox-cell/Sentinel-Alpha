# Outreach operating rules (v4.3)

Rules for **BeezShield / Sentinel Alpha** builder outreach. Read with `BUILDER_OUTREACH_PACK.md` and the tracker in `OUTREACH_TRACKER.md`.

## Conduct

- **Respectful, non-spammy:** one clear, relevant message; no mass blasts; no pressure.
- **Max one follow-up** unless they reply or explicitly ask for more (then match their pace).
- **Disclose product status truthfully** — say what is live and what is upcoming; never imply partnerships or integrations that do not exist.

## What you may say (accurate)

- **npm SDK is live:** `@beezshield/sentinel` at **0.1.0** on npm — install: `npm install @beezshield/sentinel`
- **AgentKit-style example** is available in this repo (`examples/agentkit-sentinel`); it is **not** an official Coinbase AgentKit provider integration.
- **Official provider coming next** — do not claim a live AgentKit-side provider today.

## What you must not claim

- Do not promise certainty of safety outcomes or imply a warranty of protection.
- Do not promise **automatic** settlement of x402 — builders handle payment / headers explicitly; the SDK surfaces challenges and does not auto-settle.
- Do not claim a Coinbase AgentKit provider is already live for Sentinel; the in-repo path is an **AgentKit-style example** only.

## Offers

- Offer a **small PR or integration sketch** only when it would genuinely help their codebase—never as blanket spam.

## Tracking

- Record **real** dates and outcomes only; leave cells empty until something happened.
- Use **integrated** only after you have verified they adopted Sentinel (e.g., merged PR, confirmed production usage). Never invent wins.

## Status honesty

Allowed statuses on the tracker: `not contacted`, `contacted`, `replied`, `interested`, `rejected`, `integrated`. Move statuses only on evidence.
